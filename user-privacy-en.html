1. Commitment to Privacy

IGA Factory places the highest priority on protecting the privacy of users of the Quria application (hereinafter referred to as the "App"). This document outlines our efforts to safeguard user privacy and the actions users can take.

2. Data Transparency

Principle of Transparency: We are committed to providing full transparency regarding how user data is collected and used.

2.1 Types of Data Collected

  • Learning Data: Learning progress, curriculum history, response history
  • Account Information: Email address, username (upon registration)
  • Technical Data: Device information, app usage statistics, error logs
  • Advertising Data: Advertising identifiers, ad impression and click history

2.2 Purpose of Data Use

  • Improvement of AI-generated curricula
  • Personalization of the learning experience
  • App feature improvements and bug fixes
  • Ad delivery and optimization
  • Provision of user support

3. User Privacy Controls

User Rights: Users have the following rights, which may be exercised at any time.

3.1 Right of Access

  • The right to review their own data
  • The right to inquire about how data is collected and used
  • The right to request explanations regarding data processing methods

3.2 Right of Rectification

  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request completion of incomplete data
  • The right to update account information

3.3 Right of Erasure

  • Complete deletion of an account
  • Request for deletion of specific data
  • Deletion of learning history

3.4 Right to Restrict Processing

  • The right to temporarily suspend data processing
  • The right to restrict data use for specific purposes

4. Advertising and Privacy

The App is provided as a free service, and advertisements are displayed to cover operational costs.

4.1 Advertising Data Collection

  • Use of advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID)
  • Ad impression and click history
  • Device information (for ad optimization)

4.2 Advertising Privacy Settings

  • Advertising identifiers can be disabled in device settings
  • Ad personalization can be restricted
  • Requests for deletion of advertising-related data can be made

5. Data Protection and Security

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Data encryption (during transfer and storage)
  • Access control and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Database backups

5.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Employee training on privacy
  • Minimization of data access
  • Incident response system

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for the minimum necessary period:

  • Account Information: Until account deletion
  • Learning History: Until account deletion
  • Usage Statistics: 2 years
  • Advertising Data: 1 year
  • Error Logs: 6 months

7. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not share users’ personal data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When explicit user consent has been obtained
  • When required by law
  • Minimal necessary sharing with advertising partners (for ad distribution)
  • Sharing with trusted partners for service provision

8. International Data Transfers

We primarily process data within Japan. However, international transfers may occur in the following cases:

  • Use of cloud services (with appropriate safeguards)
  • Collaboration with advertising partners (with appropriate safeguards)

9. Children’s Privacy

We place special importance on protecting children’s privacy:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If data from children is inadvertently collected, it will be promptly deleted
  • We do not process children’s data without parental consent

10. Managing Privacy Settings

Within the App, users can manage the following privacy settings:

  • Granting or denying data collection
  • Ad personalization settings
  • Settings for sending analytics data
  • Push notification settings

11. Reporting Privacy Violations

If you suspect a privacy violation, please report it through the following steps:

  1. Record detailed circumstances
  2. Save supporting evidence such as screenshots or logs
  3. Report to our contact address
  4. Consider reporting to authorities if necessary

12. Updates to Privacy Policy

We review and update this Privacy Policy regularly. Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Notifications within the App
  • Email notifications
  • Publication on our website
